Yona of the Dawn is a manga series written by Mizuho Kusanagi and serialized by Hakusensha's shojo manga magazine Hana to Yume.

The plot follows Yona, the only princess of the Kouka Kingdom. She sees her father, the King, being killed by one of her best childhood friends, and flees the palace with her guard. She is told by one of the kingdom's generals to find the help of an oracle, and when she does, she is told to seek the four dragons who will save Kouka Kingdom. The story revolves around her quest to find the dragons and help the Kingdom thrive again.
