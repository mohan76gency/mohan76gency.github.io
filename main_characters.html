Main Characters

Yona

Yona is the only princess of the Kouka Kingdom. Due to her mother's death when she was younger, she was primarily raised around her best friends, Hak and Soo-Won. However when one of them kills her father and she is forced to flee from the palace, she decides to become strong enough to protect herself.

Hak

Hak is one of Yona's childhood friends, and her bodyguard. He is an orphan but was adopted by the former Wind Tribe general, Son Mundok, and became the new general. Hak is known around Kouka as the 'Thunder Beast' for his lightning-fast reflexes and speed, and is called the strongest soldier in the kingdom.

Soo-Won

Soo-Won is Yona's cousin as well as Hak's best friend. He hides his true intelligence beneath his clumsy appearance while around others. His father was killed by the Yona's father, the king, and his anger led him to conspire to kill the king and take over the throne. Even though he has said he will stop anyone who gets in his way, his actions show he still cares about Yona and Hak.
